About Me

I am a computer engineering student who started early. Ever since my freshman year of high school I've been fascinated with computers and how they work. I have taken apart many electronics only to find out I had no idea what I was doing and couldn't put them back together, but I regret nothing. It was all worth it the moment that the first computer I have ever built started up without a problem. My eagerness to learn along with self motivation has allowed me to make computers my work, school, and hobby.

Constantly attempting to learn I often find myself researching software frameworks and technology just to spend hours trying to master them. I've tried to master things such as Android app development, web development, web design, game development, image editing, video editing, 3D modeling and simulation, and many more. It's hard to say what I'm interested in because if it involves a computer I'm interested in anything and everything. If there's a new technology out there chances are I've already researched it. I can talk your ear off about electric cars, fiber optic networks, space elevators, or even Bitcoins!

What I'm trying to say is that if you're working with something interesting and are looking for others who would be interested, I'm your guy!
